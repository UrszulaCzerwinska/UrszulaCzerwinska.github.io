Short bio

Born in Poland, I have pursued my education in France and Singapore. Currently, based in Paris where I do my Ph. D. and carry on many other projects. I have always been fascinated by complex systems.

Through my undergraduate studies I have discovered statistics and programming. Introduced since the beginning to Python and R.

Experience of living in Singapore taught me how to combine hard work, traveling and networking. It stimulated my will to work in an interdisciplinary environment which I found in Paris.

Through versatile projects I got immersed with product & graphical design, business ideas and microbiology. Since then I co-funded a start-up that got transformed into non-profit exploratory project.

I have also followed my passion for data and statistical modeling that led me to my Ph. D. Meanwhile, I have discovered the whole new universe of Data Science where my skills can serve to solve problems in business, finance, marketing and many others. I became an ambassador of Pivigo Data Science hub to spread ideas of Data Science and help other STEM students to transfer into industry.

Recently, I started running Data Science Club at the Center of Interdisciplinary Research (CRI). Our main activity is organising seminars "From Science to Data Science" where Data Science professionals holding PhD degree are welcome to tell the story of their transition from academia to private sector.

I am fascinated about Big Data, IoT and FinTech projects and Data Viz. Don't hesitate to offer me a short-term project or a job in 2018.
